Friends

Friends was a American comedy television show that ran from September 22, 1994 to May 6 ,2004.
It is about 6 friends who lived in Manhattan, New York City. They were in their 20s and 30s.

The names of the "friends" were:

  • Monica;
  • Chandler;
  • Rachel;
  • Ross;
  • Phoebe;
  • Joey.
